Our Pillars

Rapid

To excel in the Cloud, you need to be FAST. We respond fast; we implement fast; we impact fast; we problem solve fast and think fast. We believe that speed is a defining aspect of our culture.

Reliable

Being reliable means we fulfill all our commitments all the time, from simple follow up to ensuring we always do everything in our power to complete projects on-time and within budget.

Responsible

We believe that honesty, integrity and directness is not only the “right” way to conduct business, we also think it is the most productive and efficient way to drive rapid organizational growth. We choose our clients as much as they choose us, and do so based on their values and ability to execute at the pace, efficiency and level of effectiveness that we are committed to.

Relevant

The other defining aspect of our culture is relevance. Each of us have left very significant roles within larger organizations, because they cannot move fast enough in the current technology environment to “stay relevant”. We have carefully chosen our vendor relationships on the basis of their relevance to our past client relationships and the market as a whole.
